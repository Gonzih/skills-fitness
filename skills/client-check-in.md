Write a thorough client progress check-in summary based on the information provided below. The summary should be professional, motivating, and actionable.

## Client Check-In Summary

### Check-In Details
- Client name
- Check-in date and period covered (e.g., Week 4 of 12)
- Trainer / coach name

### Adherence Review
- Training sessions completed vs. planned (e.g., 3/4)
- Nutrition adherence score or qualitative rating
- Sleep and recovery quality
- Hydration habits

### Body Metrics (if tracked)
- Weight (current vs. last check-in vs. start)
- Body measurements (waist, hips, chest, arms, etc.)
- Body fat percentage (if measured)
- Progress photos notes (e.g., visible changes observed)

### Performance Wins
- Personal records (PRs) or notable improvements
- Exercises where form or confidence improved
- Non-scale victories (energy levels, mood, endurance, etc.)

### Challenges & Barriers
- Missed sessions and reason
- Nutrition slip-ups and context
- Physical complaints (soreness, fatigue, minor pain)
- Lifestyle factors (stress, travel, schedule changes)

### Trainer Assessment
- Overall progress rating (on track / ahead / behind)
- Key observations about client trajectory
- Motivation and mindset notes

### Adjustments for Next Period
- Program modifications (volume, intensity, exercise swaps)
- Nutrition adjustments or focus areas
- Behavioral or habit goals
- Specific targets for next check-in

### Motivational Closing
- Personalized encouragement based on the client's journey
- Reinforce the "why" and long-term vision
